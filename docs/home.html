Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our QR code generator.

What types of content can I encode in a QR code? expand_more
You can encode URLs, text, email addresses, phone numbers, Wi-Fi network details, and more. Our generator primarily focuses on URLs for simplicity, but advanced options are available.
Are the generated QR codes free to use? expand_more
Yes, the basic QR codes you generate are completely free for both personal and commercial use and will never expire. Advanced features like analytics and dynamic codes may require a subscription.
Can I customize the design of my QR code? expand_more
Absolutely! You can change the colors, add a logo to the center, and modify the shape of the QR code's elements to match your branding using our "Beautify" tool.
In what formats can I download the QR code? expand_more
You can download your QR codes in high-resolution PNG and SVG formats, making them suitable for both web and print applications.
